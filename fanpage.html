Bio

Tool is an American rock band from Los Angeles, formed in 1990.

A dominant act in the alternative metal scene, Tool has won four Grammy Awards, performed worldwide, and topped the charts in numerous countries.

Due to the bands use of visual arts, complex style and releases, they have also been described as psychadelic, progressive, or art rock.
